Completed Projects

Histórias Mal Contadas (DEMO)

Histórias Mal Contadas is a horror and adventure game of the narrative-driven, experimental adventure and roguelike style. You experience the journey of a Priest who wakes up in the basement of a monastery in an unknown place, with no memory and seeking answers. With few options, he ends up releasing an ancient imprisoned evil. Now, his only choice is to survive. Recovering his faith and finding answers become his main motivation.

Kaching Software

Kaching Software is a full-stack inventory and management system project organized under the MVC pattern, programmed in PHP, HTML, and CSS, with CRUD for products, employees, users, and suppliers.
